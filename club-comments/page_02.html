xm0123 | 07-30-14, 12:32 PM
Hello. I'm new here.

I guess I'll start off by talking about the last anime I watched; Little Busters EX. I've enjoyed the works of Key (both VNs and the anime adaptations) since watching Kanon 2006 several years ago. I've played the original LB VN, and watched both anime seasons. I liked them. Some things could be better, but I liked them.

Then I saw Saya's story arc in EX. I have no idea if this is, in any way, butchered from the VN. (the EX VN is still not fully translated) All I know is I saw something truly beautiful. JC Staff pulled out all the stops with the animation, Saya's VA was perfect, the story itself incorporated so many things, and incorporated them well, and this may be the best of LB I've seen yet, and that's saying something!

Also, Saya is ridiculously cute. A cute, lovely angel that breaks my heart. ;_; Whenever the 6th episode gets subbed, I'll move onto Sassasasassaasaasas-whatever. Wish me luck.
