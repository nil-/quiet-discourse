More seriously, one of the time-managing techniques is to make a list of everything you have to do before sleeping, and the next day, work on it and cross out the tasks you've taken care of. The point of making a list is that you can visualise the amount of work that you have left and see the end of it, and truly feel like you've made progress on your backlog and accomplished something when you complete tasks. It also helps you keep momentum.
