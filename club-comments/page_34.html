NavyCherub | 03-02-14, 10:22 PM
Just do everything you're obligated to do first and reward yourself with anime when you are done. If you can, make schedules as far ahead of time as possible defining exactly what you will get done on each day. Not deadlines for entire projects - then you just end up putting it off until it is too late. Do chunks over time. That way, you can do work every day and at the end of your work for that day you can spend whatever time you have left watching anime. That is what I do, anyway, and I think I'm pretty good at keeping up with stuff and not being behind on my school work.
