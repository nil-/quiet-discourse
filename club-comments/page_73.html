twinbells | 01-10-14, 6:19 PM
That stdev of 1.71 doe.

Most people here tend to favor score distributions that are heavily clustered around so most results will probably in the 4-7 range. I don't see anything breaking 7.5 to be honest.

The big difference in my opinion between this club and C-C is that discussions here have the potential to be more open-ended. When you nominate something in C-C you are saying that you feel it is "of particularly high quality and ... deserves to be added to our "Club Related" list" while here we have no such emphasis on testing works against some standard so people are less likely to come into a discussion with something to prove. As such, I haven't been nominating things that I feel are super amazing 11/10 objektiv masterpieces—agreeing about how amazing something is isn't very stimulating conversation for me, believe it or not. I've been nominating stuff like Aku no Hana and Gatchaman Crowds because I'm unsure about how I feel about many aspects them and feel that there's a lot of potential for me to get a lot out of the discussion.
